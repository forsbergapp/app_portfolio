(inner) getAngle() → {number}

Helper method. gl-vec3's angle function always returns positive but in many cases we want the angle in the direction from one vector to another. To get the sign of the angle, cross the two vectors and determine the direction the crossed vector, um, directs in. For example, the vector [0, -1, 0] would shoot negatively along the y-axis.
